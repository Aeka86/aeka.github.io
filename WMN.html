Women Of Kazakhstan is a Non-Governmental Organization that was Founded in 2016 and it highlights the achievements and challenges of Women of Kazakhstan for gender equality and empowerment. It's mission is to preserve and promote the history and historical values of Women of Kazakhstan, collect and record information on Great Women of Kazakhstan including photos and documents throughout years.

Our Mission - Women of Kazakhstan NGO is dedicated to - preserving and promoting the history and historical value of women of Kazakhstan - women empowerment. Our main goal is to collect and record information on Great Women of Kazakhstan including photos, documents, related past and present. It is a project, hobby, and dream to build and to share. The included works are meant to be a true representation of historical facts, however there may be submissions of user opinion and unverified content. All effort is taken to insure the accuracy and historical fact. The web site is under contruction. But you can look up the information on twitter(english), instagram (russian), facebook (mixed). If you would like to contribute content, memorabilia, be a guest blogger or assist in any other way please contact us.

About the Author - Dinara Assanova is a part time researcher and collector of Great Women of Kazakhstan. I donate my time, writing about women and exhibiting collections that were created back in 1950s by my grandfather Shora Sarybayev and knowledge to try and preserve history in a way others can learn and benefit from.
